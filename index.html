Course categories

The GCMR's vision is to develop the skills of men and women with active, creative minds and a sense of understanding and compassion for others. We strive to develop each student spiritually, morally, intellectually, socially, emotionally and physically. all our courses offer legitimate certificates to prove your knowledge in a given skill.

Beauty course

We offer a course that trains individuals in skills related to make-up appliance from foundation, to eyelashes, to lipstick and more, allowing you to start your own beauty salon or become a professional make-up artist.

GBV course

We strive to raise awareness about Gender Based Violence as a service to our community. In this course we educate individuals in all the forms of modern day abuse and how to help individuals in need.

Detergent & toiletries

We train individuals to create their own cleaning chemicals such as bleach, stay soft, washing powder etc. using our extensive library of formulas allowing to start you own business and outsource your products to larger enterprises.

Marriage officers course

We offer a training course to prepare you for your external final exam at home affairs in order to obtain a marriage license allowing you to carry out certified and legitimate marriage ceremonies.

Counseling course

We offer an extensive course that covers all forms of counseling and not just pre-counseling. Our course includes but is not limited to (funeral counseling, church counseling, marriage counseling etc.)

Theology leadership course

We offer a short course that comes with a higher certificate in Theology allowing you the chance to further your education to achieve greater merits such as Diploma's and even a Bachelors in Theology.

Chaplaincy course

We train individuals with the necessary communication and empathy skills required to become recognized chaplain whether for the military, churches or correctional centers.
